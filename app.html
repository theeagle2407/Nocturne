Historical Finding

Across the universe, closed hours represent ~80.7% of the week, meaning holders naturally accumulate the majority of total price movement (70.9% on average) during this window. However, per-hour volatility is consistently lower when the US market is closed than when it is open. While absolute unhedgeable exposure accumulates during closed hours simply due to the time elapsed, these tokens do not exhibit disproportionately high per-hour volatility overnight or on weekends compared to regular trading hours. Note: History is limited to the maximum ~1000 hourly candles (~41 days) available via Bitget's public API.
